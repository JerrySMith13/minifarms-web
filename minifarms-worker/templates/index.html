Helping End Food Deserts in the KC Metro

Mini Farms USA builds small-scale urban farms in underserved neighborhoods, putting fresh, healthy food back within walking distance of every family we serve.

Get Involved

What We Do

We partner with local communities to design, build, and maintain mini farms on underused lots, turning them into lasting sources of fresh produce, jobs, and neighborhood pride.

1

Build Mini Farms

We convert vacant lots into productive growing spaces designed for long-term community use.

2

Distribute Fresh Food

Produce grown on-site is distributed directly to nearby residents and area food pantries.

3

Educate & Employ

Neighbors learn to grow, harvest, and sell food, building skills and income along the way.

Join Us in Growing Change

Whether you can volunteer an afternoon, donate supplies, or spread the word, every bit of support helps a neighborhood grow its own food.
