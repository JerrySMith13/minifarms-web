Our Story

Mini Farms USA started with a single vacant lot in the Kansas City metro and a simple question: what if unused land could feed the neighborhood around it? Since then, we've grown into a network of small urban farms that turn empty spaces into sources of fresh food, jobs, and community connection.

Our Mission

We exist to end food deserts by building mini farms where they're needed most, then handing over the tools, training, and support so communities can keep them thriving for years to come.

Our Team

We're a small staff of growers, organizers, and volunteers who believe access to fresh food is a right, not a privilege. Every farm we build is designed alongside the people who will use it.
